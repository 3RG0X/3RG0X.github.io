The project's missions

Increase Comfort and Performance

Our hands and fingers need to move less. We reach the keys faster and we stress less our hand muscles, tendons and joints.

Reduce Repeptitive Strain Injuries risk

The thumb clusters and split design allow our wrist to be straight in every moment, reducing the risk of injuries such as Carpal Tunnel Syndrome.

Fully adaptable to your needs

With our configuration software you'll be able to rebind any key and create multiple layouts to adapt to your needs and habits.

Accessible for everyone

We believe that without barriers we will develop in less time a better product. The designs, electronic schematics and software will be freely available to anyone.
